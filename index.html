    PickleBall

    Pickleball is enjoyed by people of all ages and athletic abilities thanks to its ease of play and straightforward rules. The sport is inexpensive, social and healthy, and has been widely accepted in school gyms, fire stations, community centers, local parks, athletic clubs and thousands of backyard sports courts. In some ways it’s a combination of tennis and badminton, and along with sports such as table tennis and racquetball, pickleball has exploded in popularity.

    We are excited to offer you 5 indoor pickleball courts with great light and seating. Indoor bathrooms, warm up area and much more. This is a dedicated pickle ball facility. Sign up with our team reach program, Oour Team Reach code is "Premierevent"
